Policies

Cancellation Policy

Students must notify the teacher via message or email at least 24 hours before a scheduled lesson in order to reschedule the lesson for another date. This policy ensures fairness, as the teacher understands that other commitments may arise, and allows sufficient time to make necessary preparations for other students.

Refund Policy

Unused hours under the pay-as-you-go option are eligible for a refund, provided the lesson(s) are cancelled at least 24 hours in advance.

10-Lesson Bundle Packages: The 10-lesson bundle package, which includes a 10% discount, is a committed service and is therefore non-refundable. However, the lessons you purchase have no expiry date and can be used at any time in the future.

Behaviour Policy

Strictly no abusive language or violent behaviour will be tolerated from either students or parents. The teacher reserves the right to end lessons permanently at his or her discretion. Repeated misconduct may result in permanent termination of lessons without refund.

Privacy & Security

Personal information of students and their families will be kept strictly confidential. The teacher will conduct all lessons in a professional environment, and students are expected to participate from a safe, distraction-free space. Recording of lessons is not permitted without the teacher’s prior consent.
